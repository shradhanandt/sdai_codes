Leadership Narrative

📋 Executive Storyboard — March 2026 New Registrations

1

The Business is Growing at Scale

In the first 17 days of March 2026, 320,812 new non-premium domains were registered across our 12-TLD portfolio — a daily run rate of ~18,871. If sustained, this projects to over 566,000 registrations for the full month. The numbers reflect a healthy, active marketplace and strong partner distribution.

2

Three TLDs Carry the Portfolio — Opportunity in the Rest

.online (36.9%), .site (20.9%), and .store (20.4%) account for 78.2% of all registrations. This concentration is both a strength (proven demand) and a risk (single-point sensitivity). TLDs like .space (6.0%), .tech (6.7%), and .fun (3.8%) show untapped potential — targeted channel incentives or bundled promotions could diversify the base meaningfully.

3

Namecheap and Hostinger Are Our Growth Engines — GoDaddy Needs Attention

Namecheap (67,376) and Hostinger (47,545) together represent 35.8% of all volume. Hostinger in particular leads on .online — traditionally a GoDaddy stronghold — signaling a shift in channel dynamics. GoDaddy (31,781, 9.9%) remains important but is losing relative share. Reviewing GoDaddy incentive structures and co-marketing programs should be a near-term priority.

4

98% 1-Year Registrations Signal a Renewal Cliff Ahead

With 315,504 of 320,812 registrations (98.3%) being 1-year contracts, there is a large renewal event coming in Q1 2027. This is both a risk and an opportunity. Renewal rates for non-premium domains average 50–65% industry-wide. Implementing renewal nudge campaigns, multi-year discount offers, and registrar co-incentives now could materially improve the renewal conversion rate and lock in ARR.

5

166 Registrar Partners — A Long Tail Worth Nurturing

Beyond the top 5 registrars (who hold 62% of volume), there are 161 additional registrar partners contributing 38% of registrations combined. Many of these may be regional players with strong local market penetration. An enablement program for mid-tier registrars — training, co-marketing, tooling — could unlock significant untapped volume without requiring new partner recruitment.

6

Recommended Actions

① Diversify TLD promotion — increase marketing spend behind .tech, .space, .fun to reduce portfolio concentration risk.
② Partner health review — investigate GoDaddy share decline; explore deeper co-marketing with Hostinger given their growth trajectory.
③ Renewal preparedness — begin Q1 2027 renewal strategy now; target multi-year upsell at registration point.
④ Mid-tier registrar program — structured enablement to grow the long tail and reduce dependence on top 3 partners.
