## 3. Brand Archetype

Radix aligns with **Creator + Caregiver** archetypes.

### The Creator
- Dreamer and doer
- Innovative, inspiring, original
- Not afraid to go against the grain
- Proud of originality and out-of-the-box ideas

### The Caregiver
- Genuine desire to help others
- Attentive and dedicated assistance
- Looks for structure by helping others address problems, pains, and needs
- Gives customers (partners) a competitive advantage with thoughtful solutions

## 4. Brand Voice & Tone

> Voice = company's public personality (consistent).
> Tone = how personality is expressed (varies by situation).

### Voice Traits

| Trait | How to Apply |
|-------|-------------|
| **Confident** | Exude knowledge with original ideas; back everything with data & insights |
| **Inspirational** | Lead change through new solutions; show passion; aim to wow |
| **Original** | Use marketing platforms creatively; showcase out-of-the-box thinking; derive unique insights from data |
| **Empathetic** | Be inclusive to all races, genders, communities; pay attention to detail; express thoughtfulness when needed |
| **Supportive** | _(implied by Caregiver archetype)_ — assist partners, reduce friction |

### Tone Spectrum

```
Fun ←————————→ Serious
Formal ←————————→ Casual
Respectful ←————————→ Cheeky
Enthusiastic ←————————→ Matter-of-fact
```

Adjust tone based on context and emotion being conveyed.

## 5. Logo

### Refinements from Old → New Logo

Issues fixed in the 2023 logo:
1. Difficult-to-render curve at lower resolutions / small sizes
2. Unequal kerning (no optical alignment logic)
3. Angle of `X` and its disjointed stem didn't match
4. Unequal distance between top and bottom half
5. Both gap stems were unequal

### New Logo Specs
- Geometrically perfect, aesthetically refined
- Inherits boldness and confidence of predecessor
- Works better across all mediums
- Mathematically calculated kerning:
  - `R → A`: standard spacing `x`
  - `A → D`: `1.75x` (compensates for visual volume of A)
  - `D → I`: `1.5x`
  - `I → X`: standard `x`
- Angle of `X` and its stem are fully aligned; gap equals rest of alphabet spacing

### Type Logos (Unified System)
- Font: **Hubot Sans Bold Wide + Regular Wide**
- Scale easily across events / categories / verticals
- Examples: `PREMIUM DOMAINS`, `CONVERGE 2020`, `VALUES SUMMIT 2021`, `SUMMIT 2022`

## 7. Typography

### Headline Font — Hubot Sans

- **Variable typeface** with geometric accents
- Modern, bold, technical, idiosyncratic
- Perfect for headers and pull-quotes
- Designed by GitHub × Degarism
- Download: https://github.com/github/hubot-sans
- **Style in use**: Bold Wide (headlines), Regular Wide (sub-labels / type logos)

### Body Copy Font — Inter

- Variable font
- Clean, readable, modern
- Used for all body copy, captions, supporting text

## 8. Patterns

- The **X-pattern** (derived from the Radix logo) is used sparingly — only in key visuals to generate interest
- **New patterns** introduced for added visual interest — use sparingly
- Pattern colour = tint or shade of background colour
- **Data Visualisation** use: X-pattern used as graph paper background at **15% opacity**

---

## 9. Data Visualisation

- Radix is a data-centric brand — data visuals should reflect that
- Always work with **triads (3 colours)**
- All Primary and Secondary palette colours are valid
- Use family shade/tint for highlighting text and data
- Grey = third colour of the triad
- These slides/layouts should be used **sparingly** to highlight **one major data point**
